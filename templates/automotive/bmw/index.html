1995 BMW 540i 6 speed

This was my daily driver, and man was it an awesome car. The 4.0 liter V8 moved.

Mods: (not much... the 540 is pretty hot bone stock!)

  • 17" rims and low profile tires (235/45ZR-17)
  • Dinan DME chip: No speed limiter, better mileage. (before ~18mpg, now ~22mpg)
  • Dinan Induction tube (together with the chip, ~307hp)
  • Dinan Thrust arm bushings.
